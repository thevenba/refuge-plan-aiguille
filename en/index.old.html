Welcome to the Refuge du Plan de l'Aiguille

Mountain refuge at the foot of the Chamonix needles and the Mont-Blanc Massif, the Refuge du Plan de l'Aiguille overlook the vallée de Chamonix from the top of its altitude of 2207m.
Out of caretaking, the refuge keeps one room (10 beds) which is opened permanently at winter

About us

Since 2006, Claude and Marie-Noelle receive you to the Refuge du Plan de l'Aiguille, all up there, at 2207m in height where the beauty of the landscape is only matched by its richness.
